Vladimir Nabokov’s Russian translation of Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland was published in Berlin in 1923 under the title “Аня в стране чудес” (Anya v strane chudes).

• Publisher: Gamaiun (Гамаюн), a Russian émigré press in Berlin.

• Illustrations: by Sergey Sudeikin , the Russian painter and set designer associated with the Ballets Russes.
